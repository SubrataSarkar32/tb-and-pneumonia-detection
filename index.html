PneuTakeCare

The project PneuTakeCare, is an initiative by Pratyusha Sinha and Subrata Sarkar to make machine learning accessible to the every person. This model upon given the x-ray image of chest and tells whether the person has pneumonia or tuberculosis or is normal. We have three options a destop app, an android app and a webapp. We hope people are benifitted using this machine learning model.
