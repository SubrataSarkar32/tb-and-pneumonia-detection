X-ray Image Classification using Convolutional Neural Networks

Classify chest xray images for pneumonia tb and normal

We have designed and trained a convolutional neural network using keras. This will provide people with the required information before contacting the doctor and significantly speed up the process of diagnosis. We take the patient's x-ray image of the chest and then run our model and give the prediction. We have implemented a desktop app ,android app and flask web app.

This wep app has been developed by CS undergrads(Pratyusha Sinha, Subrata Sarkar) at Government College of Engineering and Ceramic Technology.
